Dedafo - Creative Agency, Studio, SaaS, Minimal Html Template

Dedafo is a fully responsive HTML5 & CSS3 template designed with a creative and clean design. Dedafo focuses on design and the simplicity to use. All the code is well commented and puredesignThemes will answer you at all your questions. This template have different layouts and is perfect for building Studio, Agencies, Freelance, SaaS, Portfolio, Corporate and Creative Websites. It comes with 20+ html ready files, 4 types of homepages and different types of Portfolio. This template is retina ready.
